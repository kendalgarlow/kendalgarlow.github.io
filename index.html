About

I am a Mohawk Nation, Wolf Clan researcher from Six Nations of the Grand River. My work is rooted in Indigenous food sovereignty, Haudenosaunee food systems, community-based research, seed governance, and climate-resilient agriculture.

Across academic, entrepreneurial, and community spaces, I am interested in how Indigenous communities protect knowledge, strengthen food systems, and build futures grounded in self-determination, care, and relational responsibility.
